datAR is an augmented reality system designed to foster data literacy in high school students. By projecting interactive data overlays onto everyday objects, datAR enables students to explore and understand data science concepts in real-world contexts. Our classroom deployment demonstrates the potential of situated learning for engaging students and enhancing their understanding of data.
